There is no "top" to the WorldWideWeb. You can look at it from many points of view, depending on your specific area of interest or technical requirements.

Navigating the Web

If you have no other bias, here are several primary methods for locating information within the web multiverse:

By Subject
A classification of information organized by subject of interest. This is the most intuitive method for new users, though the index remains an ongoing project.
By Type of Service
Searching by access protocol or service type. This method is effective if you understand the technical nature of the resource you are seeking.

Project Context

If you require a starting point or "top" node for your exploration, we recommend utilizing this overview page or the subject-based list mentioned above.
